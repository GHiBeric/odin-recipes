Douhua Fan

Description

Tofu with a chili dip, marinated vegetables and white rice.

Ingredients

  • 1tbsp. Toasted Chili Oil
  • 1.5tbsp. Soy Sauce
  • 2tsp. Salt
  • 1tsp. MSG
  • 1/4tsp. Sichuan Peppercorn Powder
  • 1/2tsp. Toasted Sesame Seeds
  • 1tbsp. Chopped Cilantro
  • 200g Cooked Celery
  • 1tbsp. Lao Gan Ma Chili Crisp
  • 1/5tsp. Rice Vinegar
  • 1/8tsp. Sugar
  • 300g Soft or Medium Tofu
  • Choice Amount of Cooked Rice

Steps

  1. Peel the ribs from the celery.
  2. Slice the celery in half, then into 4cm pieces.
  3. Blanch celery for 30 seconds, then rinse quickly under cool water.
  4. Add celery to a mason jar with the following: Seasoning: Toasted chili oil 1 tbsp; Soy sauce 1/2 tbsp; Rice vinegar 1/2 tsp; salt 1/8 tsp; sugar 1/2 tsp; MSG 1/4 tsp; toasted sesame oil 1/4 tsp
  5. Dissolve the remaining salt in the boiling water over your tofu in a bowl.
  6. Let it soak for at least 20 minutes.
  7. Drain the water, then refill with boiling water once ready to eat.
  8. Add the remaining ingredients to a small bowl and use as tofu dip.
  9. Serve immediately.
